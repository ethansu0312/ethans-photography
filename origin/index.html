Origin

I’ve always loved chasing moments, the way a city feels at night, the roar of a car, the quiet streets when nobody’s around. Photography lets me hold onto those fleeting things, the memories that would otherwise slip away. I shoot what I feel, what catches my eye, whether it’s cars, streets, or places I stumble across while traveling. Every frame is a piece of a memory, something I hope can be held onto and felt long after the shutter clicks.
